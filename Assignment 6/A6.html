ABOUT US

Founded by Terry and Gunther, Central Perk is a social dining experience inspired by the vibrancy of culture, vintage art and live music. Our aim is to provide tasty food with perfect atmosphere to hang out with your friends and family. We're passionate about our food and we hope every coffee, crispy pizza and tasty pie leaves you tongue-tied with delight!

Warning: Leave your diet at the door because our food will have you craving for more!
